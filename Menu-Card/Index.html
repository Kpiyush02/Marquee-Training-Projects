Litt-Choka

A famous Bihari dish made with stuffed wheat balls (litti) and mashed vegetables (chokha), usually served with ghee.

Thekuaa

A traditional sweet snack from Bihar made with wheat flour, jaggery, and ghee. It is crispy and popular during festivals like Chhath Puja.

Gulab-jamun

A soft and sweet Indian dessert made from milk solids, deep-fried and soaked in sugar syrup.
