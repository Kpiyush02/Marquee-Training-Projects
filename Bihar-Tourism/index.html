Sher Shah Suri Tomb

Sher Shah Suri Tomb is located in Sasaram, Bihar. It is one of the finest examples of Indo-Islamic architecture and is surrounded by a beautiful lake.

Rajgir

Rajgir is an ancient city in Bihar famous for its hot springs, Ropeway, Vishwa Shanti Stupa, and rich Buddhist and Jain heritage.

Bodh Gaya

Bodh Gaya is one of the holiest Buddhist pilgrimage sites where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment under the sacred Bodhi Tree.
